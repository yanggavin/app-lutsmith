Frequently asked questions

Is my source media uploaded?

No. LUT Smith processes photos, RAW images, and selected video frames on your device. Source media is not uploaded to LUT Smith servers.

Do I need an account?

No account is required for local LUT projects. Photos access is requested only when you choose to import from your library.

How do subscriptions work?

LUT Smith Pro is sold through the App Store. Purchases and subscription status are managed by Apple and RevenueCat. You can restore purchases from Settings.

What does a .cube LUT include?

Standard .cube LUTs store global color transforms. Preview-only render effects such as grain, masks, blur, and glow are not included unless they can be represented as global color changes.

How can I test compatibility?

Export a 17× identity LUT and one styled LUT, import both in your destination app, and confirm the identity is a no-op while the styled LUT matches your preview when input/output profiles match.
