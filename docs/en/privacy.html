Privacy Policy

LUT Smith is built around on-device LUT creation. This policy explains what LUT Smith does and does not collect.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

LUT Smith helps you design camera looks and export .cube LUT files. Projects stay on your device unless you export or share files yourself.

Media and projects

Source photos, RAW images, and selected video frames are processed on device and are not uploaded by LUT Smith. LUT projects are stored locally on your device.

Photos library access

LUT Smith requests Photos access only when you choose to import photos, RAW images, or video frames. Imported media is used for editing and export on your device.

Accounts

No account is required for local projects.

Purchases and subscriptions

LUT Smith Pro is sold through Apple's in-app purchase system. RevenueCat manages purchase and subscription status so the app can unlock Pro features and restore purchases. Purchase history may be processed for app functionality and analytics. It is not used for tracking and is not linked to your identity in LUT Smith.

On-device storage

LUT Smith uses on-device storage (UserDefaults) to remember app preferences and local project settings required for app functionality.

Tracking

LUT Smith does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Support communications

If you email support, your message may include app version and device details that we add automatically to help troubleshoot. Support emails do not include source photos, RAW images, or video frames unless you attach them.

Children's privacy

LUT Smith is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@lutsmith-app.newtree.tech.
